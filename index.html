Hospital Management System - Sequence Diagram

sequenceDiagram participant Patient participant AdminStaff participant HMS participant Doctor participant Billing %% Patient Admission Patient->>AdminStaff: Provide personal and insurance details AdminStaff->>HMS: Enter details into Patient Registration HMS->>AdminStaff: Confirm registration %% Medical Record Updates Patient->>Doctor: Provide medical issue details Doctor->>HMS: Update Medical Records with diagnosis and treatment plan HMS->>Doctor: Save confirmation HMS->>Billing: Notify billing of treatment costs %% Billing Process AdminStaff->>Billing: Generate invoice for patient Billing->>Patient: Provide invoice details Patient->>Billing: Make payment Billing->>HMS: Update payment status
